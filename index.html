When I grow up...

The begining of everything

As a child, computers were something magical that few can understand. At the age of 6, I asked for my first computer for Christmas, and with effort my parents gave me the first big dream of my life.

I didn't understand how they worked and it seemed all magic. My curiosity could not be sated with something that was so much bigger than anything my mind could imagine.

The first thing I did with the computer was open paint and draw my family. Then I drew a cat, I still remember that moment. It was the beginning of the insatiable passion for computers.

Everything changed when the internet became normal in every home. My curiosity together with a computer with internet access made it grow without limits, being able to google anything and have thousands of pages answering my craziest questions in milliseconds.

During my childhood my computer broke several times and I thought that those who knew how to repair them were super cool. A few years later, I decided that my future was related to computers. I studied computing in high school. I learned to repair them, but it was not enough to satisfy my ambitions.

After learning how to repair them, I wanted to know how to control them. Give them orders and see how what was magic before was now something tangible.

All the steps I took in my life were always surrounded by a computer and the internet. And that is precisely what led me to find Holberton and here I hope to be in May.
